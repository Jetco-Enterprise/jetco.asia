About Jetco

Local Roots. International Standards.

Founded in 2018 and headquartered in Kuala Belait, Jetco supplies and rents specialised equipment to operators and maintenance contractors across Brunei's upstream oil and gas sector.

We operate a dedicated equipment storage yard, fabrication workshop, and warehouse — giving us the operational depth to support both planned maintenance programmes and urgent breakdown requirements with short lead times.

As a Brunei-registered entity, we help contractors fulfil local content obligations while maintaining the technical standards expected on major operator sites.

Products & Services

What We Offer

We supply, rent and source equipment and materials across the disciplines required by upstream O&G maintenance contractors. Send us your requirements and we'll respond with availability and pricing.

Power & Utility

Generators, air compressors, and tower lighting for site power and utility requirements across onshore upstream operations.

Lifting & Earthmoving

Excavators, material handling equipment, and plate compactors for civil and mechanical access work within maintenance contracts.

Fluid Handling & Storage

ATEX Zone 1 rated mud tanks for sludge and drilling fluids, dewatering pumps, and associated fluid management equipment.

Fabrication & Welding

Multi-process welding systems and fabrication support equipment for onsite mechanical and structural work.

Manpower Supply

Competent personnel for operations, maintenance, and technical support roles — available on short-term and long-term mobilisation.
